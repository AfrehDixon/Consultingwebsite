    • Why Choose Us

    Accounting

    BMPT offers Accounting services such as preparation of Annual Financial Statements, Management Statements, Profit & Loss Statements, Cash Flow Statements, maintaining monthly accounting records, supplier & customer age analysis.

    Tax

    BMPT delivers Tax services such as SARS Tax Compliance, VAT, Company Income Tax, Personal Income Tax, Provisional Tax, PAYE, UIF calculation & submission of returns, change of tax payers' details on eFiling, load of payment on eFiling.

    CIPC & Registrations

    BMPT completes the following registrations/ changes: Company (CIPC) registrations, Income Tax, VAT, PAYE, UIF,Compensation Fund registrations, CIPC amendments- change in directors or address etc.

    Other Submissions

    BMPT completes the following other calculations & submissions: CIPC Annual Returns, CIPC Financial Accountability Supplement, CIPC Annual Compliance Checklist, Compensation Fund Annual ROE.

    Professional Advice

    Receive advice from one of our Accountants or Tax Practitioners on financial and tax management.

    Accounting Officer

    BMPT prepares documents required from the client's Accounting Officer by third parties. Including Professional Letters, Management/ Financial Statements, Certified Documents etc.
